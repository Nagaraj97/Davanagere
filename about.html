Smart City ♥ Davanagere :

Davanagere is a bustling city located in the heart of Karnataka, India. It is known for its cotton textile industries, educational institutions, and mouth-watering cuisine. The city's economy is driven by agriculture, with crops like paddy, sugarcane, and cotton being the mainstay. The city is also home to several reputed educational institutions, including the Bapuji Institute of Engineering and Technology and the University of Agricultural Sciences. Davanagere is famous for its culinary delights, with the benne dose (butter dosa) being a must-try for any food lover visiting the city. The city's rich cultural heritage is reflected in its many festivals, including the popular Dasara festival celebrated with much pomp and show.

    Davanagere has several tourist attractions, including:
  • Kunduvada Kere: A picturesque lake located on the outskirts of the city, popular for boating and picnics.
  • Harihar: A historic town near Davanagere, known for its ancient temples like the Harihareshwara temple.
  • Bheemalingeswara temple: A popular Shiva temple located in the heart of the city, known for its intricate architecture.
  • Aanekonda: A serene village located near Davanagere, known for its natural beauty and the Aanjaneya temple.
  • Textile industries: Davanagere is known for its cotton textile industries, and visitors can explore the local markets purchase some of the finest cotton products.
  • Benne Dosa: A famous local delicacy, the butter dosa, is a must-try for food lovers visiting Davanagere.
  • Kundadri Hill: A beautiful hill located near Davanagere, known for its scenic views and the ancient Jain temple.
  • Sathya Sai Grama: A spiritual retreat located near Davanagere, dedicated to the teachings of Sri Sathya Sai Baba.
  • Durgambika Temple: A popular temple located in the city, dedicated to the Hindu goddess Durga.
  • Keladi: A historic village near Davanagere, known for its ancient temples and monuments.
